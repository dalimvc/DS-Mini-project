Cluster Profiling

    Cluster 0
  • This cluster represents 9.7% of all customers.
  • They have the second-highest average income, around $70,000.
  • They are the top spenders on wine (average $600) and gold (average $75).
  • They rank second in spending on meat, fruits, sweets, and fish, just behind Cluster 2.
  • They visit our website less frequently (average 5 visits) than other clusters, except Cluster 2, but lead in web purchases with an average of 7 purchases.
  • They are the top group for in-store purchases, averaging 10, and second for catalog purchases, averaging 5.
  • Most customers in this cluster have teenagers at home, with minimal presence of younger children.
    Cluster 1 and Cluster 4
  • These two clusters share many similarities with a few notable differences.
  • Cluster 1 comprises almost one-third of all customers 28.7%, while Cluster 4 is much smaller, representing 7.5% of all customers.
  • Customers in these clusters have the lowest average income among all groups, around $30,000.
  • They spend the least across all product categories.
  • They visit our website more frequently than other clusters (average 7 visits), but they rarely purchase online, averaging 1 purchase for Cluster 1 and 2 for Cluster 4.
  • Both clusters have the lowest number of store purchases, with Cluster 1 averaging 2 purchases and Cluster 4 averaging 3.
  • Catalog purchases are almost nonexistent in these clusters, averaging 0.
  • Most customers in these clusters have children at home, with Cluster 4 also showing a significant proportion having teenagers.
  • Cluster 1 has a majority of customers with only secondary education, while their marital status is similar to customers in other clusters.
    Cluster 2
  • This is our second-largest cluster, comprising nearly 19% of all customers.
  • Customers in this cluster have the highest average income, surpassing $80,000.
  • They are among the top spenders across all product categories.
  • They lead in meat expenditure, with an average of $420, and are top spenders on fruits, fish, and sweets.
  • They rarely purchase discounted products, with an average of just 1 purchase, significantly less than other clusters.
  • They visit our website the least, averaging only 2 visits.
  • Most purchases are made via catalog or in-store.
  • A majority of customers in this cluster do not have children, and most have tertiary education.
    Cluster 3
  • This cluster comprises 9.6% of all customers.
  • Customers in this group have the third-highest average income, exceeding $60,000.
  • They rank third in spending on fruits ($25), meat ($180), fish ($25), and sweets ($25).
  • They are the fourth-largest group in wine spending (average $500) and gold spending (average $40).
  • They visit our website an average of 5 times, making them average in this behavior, but they rank third in web purchases (average 6), in-store purchases (average 9), and catalog purchases (average 3).
  • A majority of customers in this cluster do not have young children but have teenagers at home.
    Cluster 7
  • This is the smallest cluster, representing just 5.1% of all customers.
  • Customers in this group have the fourth-highest average income, slightly below $60,000.
  • They are tied with Cluster 2 as the second-highest spenders on wine, averaging just under $600.
  • They spend very little on fruits, meat, fish, and sweets, with meat averaging $100 and other categories under $25.
  • They are the second-highest spenders on gold, averaging over $50.
  • They are the most likely to buy discounted products, with an average of 5 purchases.
  • Alongside Clusters 4 and 5, they visit the website most frequently, averaging 7 visits.
  • They are among the most likely to purchase online, averaging 7 web purchases.
  • Most customers in this cluster do not have young children but almost all have teenagers at home.
    Cluster 5
  • This cluster represents 9.6% of all customers.
  • Customers in this group have an average income of $45,000.
  • They have low spending across all product categories.
  • They are the second-most likely group to purchase discounted products, averaging 4 purchases.
  • They visit our website most frequently, with an average of 7 visits.
  • Most customers in this cluster have young children, but a large majority also have teenagers at home.
  • They make an average of 4 purchases each via the web and in-store.
    Cluster 6
  • This cluster represents 10.9% of all customers.
  • Customers in this group have an average income of $50,000.
  • They have low spending across all product categories.
  • They rarely purchase discounted products.
  • They have low purchase activity across all channels.
  • They visit our website infrequently, averaging 5 visits.
  • Most customers in this cluster do not have young children, but the majority have teenagers at home.
